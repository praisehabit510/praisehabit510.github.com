Sports

I have done a variety of sports in my life, but I've settled on a few.

Eventing

This is by far my favorite sport. I've ridden horses for a long time, but only in the past few years have I started eventing, which is a combo of dressage, cross country, and show jumping.

Swimming

I started swimming in 2008, and swam quite frequently with my local USA Swim team for roughly four years. A few years ago I quit the swim, due to shoulder injury. I still love swimming though, I just (unfortunately) am unable to do it as often as I'd like.

Diving

Once I started swimming for my local high school, I was informed that they had hired a full time dive coach. I decided to try it, and for a time, liked it better than swimming, but in the past year I realized that I do enjoy eventing the most.
