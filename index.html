About

SGE is a simple farm. Our colors are navy and yellow, and we are in the USEA's Area 3. We often show at Poplar Place Farm, Rocking Horse Stables, Florida Horse Park, or, less often, Chatt Hills Eventing.

Phenomenon

Pheeps, our main horse, is a bay quarter horse that moves like a little Warmblood pony. Unfortunately he has been diagnosed with Navicular's disease, so until we have some spare time, he is currently not showing. We hope that he will be back in the shows soon, we are looking at de-nerving part of his hoof so he doesn't feel pain.
